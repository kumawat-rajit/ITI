Welcome to Rooprajat ITI

Bhushan Pvt. ITI,Jaipur has been setup with the sole objective of imparting vocational  training to aspiring technicians  offering an environment where young  minds can  grow to  their full  potential and become disciplined and skilled  Technicians.

The institutes is also  striving  for reducing unemployment among educated youth by equipping them with suitable skills for employment and satisfy the requirements  of Industries  both for manufacturing  and service  sectors. Quality Training and 100% Placement support has been key factors in making Bhushan ITI as the Best ITI in Jaipur.

This is matter  of pride not  only for  the Jaipur  but also Rajsthan  state that Bhushan Pvt. ITI made it’s place among Top Ten Private ITIs in India. It got 9th Rank in India and 2nd Rank in Rajasthan State.

The  Directorate  General  of  Training,  Ministry of  Skill  Development  and Entrepreneurship recognized the performance of the Top 10 Government and Top 10  Private ITIs  according  to the National  level ITI  Grading concluded recently.  In order  to commensurate  the  performance,  Honorable Minister Petroleum, Skill  Development  and Entrepreneurship  Mr. Dharmendra Pradhan acknowledged the outstanding performance through a Felicitation program on  the occasion  of  Teachers’ Day  on 5th September 2018. by awarding Certificates to Mr. N. K. Soni, Principal Bhushan Pvt, ITI,

This achievement by institute is result of untiring efforts by the Teachers, staff and Placement Cell of Institute who performed their best to impart quality training and supported the trainees in getting suitable employment.
